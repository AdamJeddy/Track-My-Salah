Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

TL;DR: TrackMySalah does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We collect nothing. TrackMySalah does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information, prayer records, or usage data on external servers.

2. How Your Data is Stored

All prayer tracking data, including:

  • Prayer completion records (On-Time, Qadha, Missed)
  • Personal settings and preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Gender preferences (for prayer calculation purposes)

...is stored locally on your device only using browser storage technologies (IndexedDB via LocalForage). This data never leaves your device.

3. Data Sharing

We don't share your data because we don't have access to it. Your information is stored exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

4. Notifications

If you enable daily reminder notifications:

  • Reminders are scheduled locally on your device
  • No notification data is sent to external servers
  • You can disable notifications at any time in Settings

5. Third-Party Services

TrackMySalah does not use:

  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud synchronization services
  • Backend servers or databases

6. Data Export and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: Use the export feature in Settings to download your prayer records as a JSON file
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app or clearing your browser data will permanently delete all stored information

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. All data remains local to the device.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Open Source

TrackMySalah is built with privacy by design. You can verify our claims by reviewing the source code.
